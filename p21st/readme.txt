Prisoners in 2021 – Statistical Tables  NCJ 305125	
	
This zip archive contains tables in individual  .csv spreadsheets	
from Prisoners in 2021 – Statistical Tables  NCJ 305125. The full report including text	
and graphics in pdf format is available from: https://bjs.ojp.gov/library/publications/prisoners-2021-statistical-tables	
	
This report is one in a series.  More recent editions may be available.  To view a list of all in the series go to	
https://bjs.ojp.gov/library/publications/list?series_filter=Prisoners
